CYOA Project

This Project was about making our own little mini game adventure with our peers!

Our Teacher assigned this project because he wanted us to work together, help eachother overcome obsticles and for us to learn how to connect folders to folders and much more.

One of the challenges me and my peers faced was communication. Due to COVID-19 we are doing remote learning and are no longer going in to the school building so it was hard to communicate but we over came that by staying after class and finishing our work as well as having eachother numbers so that we know when to pull and push into github!
